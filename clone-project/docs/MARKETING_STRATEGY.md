# Marketing Strategy - InstantMeet

## Positioning
- **Tagline:** Get paid before you meet. Route every lead to the right teammate.
- **Promise:** Cut no-shows, collect revenue upfront, and auto-route demos without admin overhead.
- **ICP:** Agencies/consultants, sales teams, CS/onboarding teams.

## Channels
- Product Hunt launch (today)
- Hacker News “Show HN”
- LinkedIn + Twitter threads by founders/early users
- Outbound to agency/consultant lists with templates
- SEO: “paid discovery calls”, “round robin scheduler”, “paid meeting link”

## Growth Loops
- Viral: Booking confirmation shares organizer’s referral link for teammates.
- PLG: Free/Starter plan lets users activate instantly; upgrade for routing/webhooks.
- Templates: Pre-built booking pages for agencies, demo teams, CS onboarding.

## Metrics
- Activation: first meeting type created + first booking link shared
- Conversion: paid checkout initiated on booking link
- Retention: weekly bookings per organizer
- Expansion: upgrades to Pro/Team, additional workspaces
