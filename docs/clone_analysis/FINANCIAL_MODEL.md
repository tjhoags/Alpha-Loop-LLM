# Financial Model (12-Month Directional) - InstantMeet

## Assumptions
- ARPU blended: $38/mo (mix of Starter/Pro/Team).
- Gross margin: ~85% (infra + payment processing fees).
- Paid conversion from trials: 8%.
- Monthly churn: 4%.
- CAC: $35 (Product Hunt + SEO + light paid retargeting).
- Payback: ~1.1 months at blended ARPU.

## Month 3 (Target)
- 500 trials, 40 paid → ~$1.5k MRR.
- Focus on agencies/consultants; PH launch + outbound to agency lists.

## Month 6 (Target)
- 2,500 trials, 200 paid → ~$7.6k MRR.
- Add templates, webhooks, and team routing upsell.

## Month 12 (Target)
- 6,000 trials, 520 paid → ~$19.8k MRR.
- Expand to CS/onboarding teams; partner with CRM resellers.

## Breakeven
- Fixed costs: ~$800/mo (hosting, email, monitoring, minimal marketing tools).
- Breakeven at ~25 Pro accounts or ~45 Starter accounts.

## Cash Efficiency
- PLG-first; CAC payback ~1 month keeps burn low.
- Upsell levers: routing, enrichment, webhooks, extra workspaces.
