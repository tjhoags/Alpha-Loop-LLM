# Pricing Strategy: InstantMeet

## Plan Structure
- **Starter – $19/mo**
  - 1 calendar, 1 payment flow
  - Basic branding
  - Email notifications

- **Pro – $49/mo**
  - Team routing (round-robin, priority)
  - Advanced branding + custom domain
  - Webhooks + CRM export
  - Lead enrichment (basic)

- **Team – $99/mo**
  - Multiple workspaces
  - Audit logs and access control
  - Priority support
  - Enhanced lead enrichment

## Add-ons
- Usage-based overages on booked meetings above monthly quota.
- Additional workspaces ($20/mo each).

## Rationale
- Price above commodity schedulers by focusing on payments-first value.
- Clear ROI: fewer no-shows, paid discovery calls, faster lead routing.
- PLG-friendly Starter; expansion via routing, enrichment, and webhooks.
